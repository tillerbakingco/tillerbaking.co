What is Tiller Baking?

Here's What We're About

Tiller Baking Co. is a wholesale bakery serving Charleston, South Carolina. We make hand-crafted, organic, sourdough breads using only grains, water, and salt.

Farmer's Market

Tuesdays at the Farmer's Market

We sell our breads on Tuesdays at the Mount Pleasant Farmers Market.

Tiller Bakery Home Delivery?

Out bread comes to your house.

We are partners with Community Supported Grocery and our breads are available as a part of their home grocery delivery service.

Come on Over and Pickup bread!

We are open for pick ups between 8am and 11am Tuesday through Friday.

We need one week advance notice for orders. Orders need to be submitted via email to retail@tillerbaking.co For any other inquiries and to find out about being a wholesale customer fill out our contact form. Thanks!
